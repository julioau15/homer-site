    HOMER SIMPSON

    Homer

    Homer Simpson is one of the main characters from the animated TV show The Simpsons. He is the father of the Simpson family and works at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Homer is known for being lazy, clumsy, and loving donuts, beer, and watching TV. Even though he often makes mistakes, he deeply loves his family and tries his best to take care of them. His famous catchphrase is “D’oh!”
