    SPRINGFIELD SCHOOL

    Springfield School

    Springfield Elementary School is the notoriously underfunded and highly dysfunctional educational institution attended by Bart, Lisa, and most of Springfield's children. It is constantly depicted as being on the verge of collapse due to neglect and a lack of funding. The school is run by the perpetually harassed Principal Seymour Skinner and overseen by the easily exasperated Superintendent Chalmers.
