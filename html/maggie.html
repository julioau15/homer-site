Simpsons Character

MAGGIE SIMPSON

Maggie

Maggie Simpson is the silent, pacifier-sucking baby of the family. Though she rarely speaks (her first official word was "Daddy"), she is a surprisingly observant and intelligent character, once revealed to have an IQ of 167. Maggie often expresses herself through subtle actions, displaying both sweetness and a surprising level of heroism.
