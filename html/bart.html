Simpsons Character

BART SIMPSON

Bart

Bart Simpson is the rebellious and mischievous 10-year-old son from The Simpsons. Famous for his catchphrases like "Ay, caramba!", he is the quintessential underachiever and prankster. Bart is constantly at odds with authority, especially Principal Skinner and his father, Homer. Despite his bad-boy antics, he occasionally shows a good heart, making him one of television's most iconic and enduring characters.
