MARGE SIMPSON

Marge

Marjorie "Marge" Simpson is the kind, patient, and long-suffering matriarch of the family, easily recognizable by her impossibly tall blue hair. She is the moral and emotional anchor that constantly tries to keep her chaotic family grounded. As a dedicated homemaker, Marge often puts aside her own dreams and talents (like painting or bowling) to care for Homer, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie. She possesses a strong moral compass and a surprising inner strength. Despite her occasional frustrations—usually expressed with a signature throaty groan—Marge remains fiercely devoted to her husband and children, proving time and again that she is the beating heart of the Simpsons household.
